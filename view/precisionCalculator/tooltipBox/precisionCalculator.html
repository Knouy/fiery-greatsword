Precision Calculator

Designed for Fractals of the Mists, the precision calculator allows you to optimize your chances of critical hits without exceeding a precision threshold.
It takes into account:
• Agony Impedance    • Fractal Attunements
• Attributes         • Gizmos
• Boons              • Mist Attunements
• Conditions         • Professions
• Consumables        • Sigils
• Effects            • Traits
• Equipment          • Utility Skills
